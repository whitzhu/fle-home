KA Lite Around the World
A map of known installations and deployments of KA Lite curated by the Learning Equality team

When KA Lite was first released in December of 2012, we tried to track known installations and budding partnerships on a map on the wall of our office.

As the trickle of installations turned into a flood, our wall map quickly became outdated. Over the past year and half, we've been in touch with hundreds of individuals and organizations using KA Lite, and we decided that it was time for more of these stories to be shared with the world.

The red pins on the map are individuals or organizations who have downloaded KA Lite, installed it, and registered with our central server. The green houses represent individuals or organizations with whom we have been in contact (by email, phone, or in-person meetings), who have been using KA Lite to enhance educational opportunities for the communities that they serve. The stories and photos included here were generously supplied by these incredible people.

Check out the about section of the Learning Equality website to learn more about our our team and mission. Looking for more in depth stories and news? Visit our blog! Don't forget to subscribe!
