Directions to the Learning Equality Offices

The Learning Equality offices are located in the Qualcomm Institute Innovation Space, in Richard C. Atkinson Hall, on the UCSD Campus.

Visitors to the UCSD campus are allowed to park in Visitor spaces after purchasing a parking permit from one of the automated machines found at each parking lot ($1 / 45 minutes, $8 for a full-day). Visitor spaces are marked with a white "V". You can read more about parking at UCSD on the Parking & Transportation Website.

The most convenient Visitor spots near our offices are on Equality Lane, in Warren College, across from Richard C. Atkinson Hall. A red pin on the map below marks Equality Lane.

{# Iframe of Atkinson Hall on Map #}

If those spaces are full, you should be able to find space in Hopkins Parking Structure which is just up the hill and marked with a red pin in the embedded map below.

Once you have successfully parked, please contact the Learning Equality team member that you are scheduled to meet with and they will either meet you at the base of Atkinson Hall or provide you with further directions. Richard C. Atkinson Hall is marked with a red pin in the embedded map below.
