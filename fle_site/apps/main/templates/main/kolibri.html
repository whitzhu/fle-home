About Kolibri

Kolibri is an ecosystem of hardware, software, people, and content that empowers educators of all types to seamlessly bridge the online and offline worlds. The Kolibri ecosystem relies on non-governmental organizations like UNICEF, content curators like Khan Academy, teachers, and learners to enable everyone to access, share, and create rich educational content. Kolibri is still a vision in progress, and with your help, we can contribute to the ecosystem to make it a reality.
